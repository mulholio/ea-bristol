# Doing the Most Good We Can

How can we make the world a better place? Which charities do the most good? What are the biggest risks facing humanity and what can we do about it?

Effective Altruism is a philosophy and social movement that applies evidence and reason to determining the most effective ways to improve the world.

## Want to learn more?

If you're new to Effective Altruism, there are loads of great resources to learn more about it:

- [effectivealturism.org](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/) - The official EA site
- [Wikipedia's entry on Effective Altruism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Effective_altruism)
- [80,000 Hours](https://80000hours.org/) - A set of resources on having a high-impact career
- [Will MacAskill's TED talk on EA](https://www.ted.com/talks/will_macaskill_how_can_we_do_the_most_good_for_the_world?language=en)
- [The Most Good You Can Do](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Most-Good-You-Can-Effective/dp/0300180276) by Peter Singer
- [Doing Good Better](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/1783350512/ref=asc_df_178335051257514244/?tag=googshopuk-21&creative=22110&creativeASIN=1783350512&linkCode=df0&hvadid=311043780415&hvpos=1o3&hvnetw=g&hvrand=4071899468241871841&hvpone=&hvptwo=&hvqmt=&hvdev=c&hvdvcmdl=&hvlocint=&hvlocphy=9045628&hvtargid=pla-471330494535&th=1&psc=1) by Will MacAskill
- If you'd rather talk to a person than read a book,  just come along to one of our socials and ask some questions!
